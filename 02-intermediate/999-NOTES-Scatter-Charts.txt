SCATTER CHARTS are good for displaying the relationship between two different data types.

SCATTER CHARTS are ideal for two columns of data.

An example of data good for a scatter chart:
income & life expectancy

When creating LINE CHARTS if you select the "column headers" of each column, then your line chart might not create well.

When creating SCATTER CHARTS if you select the "column headers" of each column, your scatter chart will be just fine.

You can choose different designs for your chart easily.
CHART TOOLS / DESIGN

The way you chart your data influences the message that is conveyed.

The order of your columns of data impacts the way your scatter chart is created.

The "Switch Row/Column Data" trick doesn't work for scatter charts.

There are many different charts which you can create, like AREA STACKED CHARTS
